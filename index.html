Welcome to Park Lane Methodist Church

It is our pleasure to welcome you to Park Lane Methodist Church here in the heart of Wembley.

Our vision is to continue being a welcoming and caring Church within the community. To nurture and equip our community in their Christian discipleship, prepare the church for mission and evangelism at local and wider levels and to resource and equip lay leadership in the church.

Join us for service every Sunday from 10:30am.
