About our Church

The vibrant "Church on the Hill" serving the Wembley community since 1912.

Our Vision

"For everyone to know Jesus"

Our Mission

To inspire everyone to become disciples by applying the principles of the Bible to their personal lives. We strive to nurture and equip our community in their Christian discipleship, preparing the church for mission and evangelism at local and wider levels.

Life at Park Lane Methodist Church

If this is your first visit to the Church or our website, we would like a chance to get to know you. On your visit, please sign the visitor's book in the vestibule of the Church.

The congregation meets every Sunday for worship and during the week for many social, youth events, and biblical meetings. On Sundays, there is also a crèche for babies and young children. Our Stewards are on duty every Sunday and are always available to assist with any aspect of the Church.

All our services now utilize the latest audio/visual projection technology to enhance our worship experience.
